Welcome to TeamFlow AI

Use the chat assistant to manage tasks and schedules!

TeamFlow is a clever virtual assistant that transforms how you work in Microsoft Teams by making everything simpler and smarter. It pulls together your chats from Teams, emails from Outlook, tasks from Planner, and files from OneDrive into one easy dashboard, so you don't have to hunt for stuff across different apps. With its Azure AI magic, it spots things like "Let's meet tomorrow" in your messages and instantly sets up a meeting, assigns tasks, and sends reminders—no extra work needed! It also keeps an eye out for problems, popping up alerts like "John hasn't shared the data yet!" to keep your projects on track. Plus, it syncs with apps outside Microsoft, like Slack and Zoom, using Power Automate, so all your tools play nice together. It's like having a super-organized friend who handles the boring stuff, leaving you free to shine!
